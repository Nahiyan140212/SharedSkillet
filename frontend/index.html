Shared Skillet Business Plan

1. Vision & Mission:
Our goal is to make cooking more accessible, fun, and culturally enriching.
Vision: Become the leading online platform for traditional and modern recipes with AI-powered cooking assistance.

2. Business Model:
- **Recipe Upload & AI Cooking Assistant**: Users can upload and share their own recipes.
- **Food Image Recognition & LLM Integration**: AI suggests recipes based on uploaded images.
- **On-Demand Cooking & Delivery**: Catered food delivery for special events.

3. Revenue Streams:
- **Subscription Plans** for exclusive AI-powered cooking guides.
- **Catering Services** with local chefs and meal preps.
- **Affiliate Marketing** through kitchen tools and ingredient partnerships.

4. Market Strategy:
- Leverage **SEO & Social Media** to attract home cooks and food lovers.
- Collaborate with **YouTube & Facebook food influencers** to promote content.
- Use **AI-driven recommendations** to engage users with personalized meal ideas.

5. Technology Stack:
- **Frontend**: TypeScript, React, Tailwind CSS.
- **Backend**: Python (FastAPI/Django), PostgreSQL, AWS Lambda.
- **AI/ML**: Hugging Face models for LLM-based cooking suggestions.
- **Cloud Infrastructure**: AWS S3, Route 53, CloudFront for hosting.

6. Competitive Edge:
- **AI-powered Recipe Assistant** ‚Äì Unlike traditional cooking websites, we offer interactive AI guidance.
- **Cultural Authenticity** ‚Äì Recipes from diverse backgrounds, including personal family favorites.
- **Community Engagement** ‚Äì Encouraging users to share and modify recipes.

7. Roadmap:
- **Phase 1:** Launch MVP with AI recipe assistant & image recognition.
- **Phase 2:** Introduce premium subscription plans.
- **Phase 3:** Expand into catering & delivery partnerships.
- **Phase 4:** Scale using advanced AI models for personalized nutrition recommendations.
