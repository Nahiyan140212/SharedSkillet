Chocolate mud cake

Recipe - Make butter milk by adding lemon juice in a cup of milk. Mix 1 tsp instant coffee powder with a cup of hot water. In a pan add 250 gm dark chocolate , the prepared coffee, 1/2 cup of oil/butter and melt them altogether. For the dry mix sift 2 cup all purpose flour, 1/2 cup cocoa powder, 2 tspn baking soda, 1 tspn baking powder. Add 1 and 1/2 cup of sugar / sugar substitute ( Definitely better to use sugar if you can compromise with your diet, otherwise you have to compromise with taste like me ). Now with the melted chocolate add the prepared buttermilk, 2 room temperature eggs and whisk together. Add the liquid mix with the dry one and form a nice, smooth batter. Butter/oil brush a pan, pour the batter and bake at 160 c / 325 F preheated oven for 40 minutes or longer. Demould it,brush some sugar syrup.
