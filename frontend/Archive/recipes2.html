Chicken Roast

Biye bari style chicken roast. Recipe - Marinate leg quarters with red chilli powder and salt. Shallow fry them. In the same oil fry some onion and whole spices untill the onions turn brown. Then add ginger garlic paste, onion and almond paste. Add red chilli powder , turmeric powder, Cumin-Coriander powder, Garam masala and mix nicely. Add youghurt and cook untill the oil separates. Now, add the chicken. Then add black pepper, green chilli, garam masala, milk powder, raisins, kewra water, salt. Lastly, add fried onion and cook untill the gravy thickens

Butter garlic toast

Butter Garlic Toast. Recipe- Fry some garlic,mash them. Then add cilantro, chilli flakes and butter. Spread the mix over bread. Grate some mozzarella cheese over it. Sprinkle some permasean cheese over the bread. (You may skip it). Airfry at 400 degree farenheit for 5/6 min

Chicken 65 biriyani

Chicken 65 biriyani. Recipe - Marinate chicken cubes with beaten egg, corn starch, ginger garlic paste, red chilli powder, cumin-coriander powder, garam masala, black pepper, baking powder, salt and pinch of orange food color (forgot to add the clip) Now fry them untill crispy. In another pan fry onion untill golden brown. Now in a pan take oil and sautee some green chilli, curry leaves, cilantro, garlic cloves and tomatoes. Then add ginger garlic paste, the fried onion. Now add red chilli powder ,turmeric powder, cumin coriander powder, garam masala. Add beated yoghurt and a paste of onion-almond-cumin. Add salt and cook by covering. When the oil separates add half of the fried chicken. Meanwhile, boil rice in another pot and when done do the layering as your preference. Now cook im dum for another 20 min in medium low heat

Kofta biriyani

Chicken Kofta Biriyani. A mild, less spicy yet delish biriyani for those who can't handle too much spicy. Recipe - Make kofta by making a keema with chicken, onion, green chilli, ginger garlic paste, salt, red chilli powder, cumin-coriander powder, garam masala and corn flour. Shape them into balls and airfry. Now make a paste with onion, green chilli, almond , raisin and poppy seed. In a pan take oil. Once hot add whole spices, onion and sautee till the onion turns brown. Now, add the paste and beaten youghurt,green chilli.Add garam masala, any biriyani masala ( I used radhuni hydrabadi briyani masala), kewra water, rose water, ginger garlic paste and black pepper.Cook untill the oil separates. Then add pre soaked rice, mix well with the gravy. Lastly add proper amount of hot water, salt, liquid milk and ghee.When the rice is halfway done add the chicken balls, cover and let it cook untill the water is reabsorbed. Garnish with some fried onions, cashews and raisins.
