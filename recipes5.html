Tandoori fish

Tandoori Pomfret. Recipe - Make a marinade with youghurt, ginger-garlic paste, pinch of food color, kashmiri red chilli powder, garam masala, cumin-coriander powder, black pepper, lemon juice, salt. Take some mustard oil in a pan, warm it up and add some chilli powder in it. Mix the chilli oil in the marinade. Marinate the pomfrets with the marinade. Bake at 350 farenheit for 50 min and broil for some minutes.

Steam fish

Thai style steamed snapper with a desi touch. - Make a marinade with lemon juice,ginger garlic paste,olive oil,soya sauce,black pepper,lemon pepper seasoning, salt,garlic powder,onion powder,bbq sauce, shrimp bullion,diced onion,capsicum,mint leaves,green chilli,red chilli and diced tomatoes. Marinate the fish with the mix wrap it with a foil paper.Now bake at 400 farenheit for 30-40 minute!

Tuna kebab

Tuna kebab. Recipe - Mix 1 mashed potato, 1 can of tuna(strain the water), coriander, green chilli, fried onion, ginger garlic paste, coriander powder, garam masala, red chilli powder, black pepper, corn flour, bread crumb, salt and 1 egg yolk all together. Give them shape. Now coat them firstly into egg white and then into bread crumbs. Fry them nicely untill golden brown
